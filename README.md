# Gym-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Gym Management Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution designed to analyze and monitor gym performance, membership growth, revenue, expenses, and profitability.

This dashboard provides a complete overview of gym operations, helping management track financial performance, monitor active and expired memberships, and evaluate monthly trends. The goal of this project is to convert operational data into actionable insights for better strategic and financial decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following core metrics:

- 👥 Total Members: 100  
- 🏋️ Total Trainers: 20  
- 💰 Total Revenue: 4M  
- 💸 Total Expenses: 1M  
- 📈 Total Profit: 3M  
- 🗓 Monthly Membership Growth  
- 🥇 Active & Expired Membership Distribution  

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Financial Performance Analysis
- Monthly revenue, expenses, and profit comparison  
- Overall profit monitoring  
- Revenue vs expense trend tracking  

### 2️⃣ Membership Analysis
- Active vs expired memberships  
- Membership distribution by category (Platinum, Gold, Silver)  
- Remaining membership days tracking  

### 3️⃣ Category-wise Performance
- Performance comparison between Platinum, Gold, and Silver plans  
- Revenue contribution by membership type  

### 4️⃣ Monthly Growth Analysis
- New member additions per month  
- Seasonal membership trends  
- Identification of peak enrollment months  

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Platinum memberships contribute significantly to overall revenue despite lower volume.
- Monthly trends show fluctuations in new member registrations, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
- Profit margins remain strong due to controlled expense management.
- A noticeable number of memberships are approaching expiration, highlighting opportunities for renewal campaigns.
- Silver memberships have higher volume but lower revenue contribution compared to premium plans.

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

- Enables better financial planning by tracking revenue, expenses, and profit trends.
- Supports targeted membership renewal strategies.
- Helps optimize pricing strategy across membership categories.
- Assists management in forecasting monthly revenue growth.
- Improves operational efficiency by monitoring trainer-to-member ratio.
- Provides data-driven insights for marketing and promotional campaigns.

This dashboard transforms gym operational data into actionable business intelligence, improving profitability and strategic planning.
